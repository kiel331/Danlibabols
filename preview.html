Why D Anbilibabols?

We are Electrical Engineering students from Bicol University building practical technology for local communities. Instead of depending entirely on internet infrastructure, we designed a hardware-first solution that keeps working when conditions are at their worst.


Our mission is simple: provide faster flood warnings, reduce panic, and help protect families across flood-prone areas in Albay.
